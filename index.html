ABOUT US


   GUIDANCE GROUP is a committed team of faculty who have been involved in Civil Service preparation for the past "5-6 YEARS" and have been instrumental for the success of many candidates in their Civil Service exam Preparation.
   We offer intensive and quality coaching to candidates appearing for Civil Service Examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission and the State Service Examination.

Scholarship

YES, We offer it!

We provide every candidate with a scholarship in one of our courses. The candidate if interested for the same has to enroll for an entrance test at our office which will be evaluated in a day's time. After getting a satisfactory result the candidate will be allotted sufficient discounts to enroll for any of the course of their choice.

Special Scholarship

Special Scholarship for students from economically backward class. To avail for this the candidate has to provide necessary documents. Depending upon that the candidate will be allotted scholarships.
